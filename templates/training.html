Assessment & Certification

At the end of Day 5, you'll complete a practical assessment that covers:

  • Incident response and triage procedures
  • Tool proficiency (Jira, ServiceNow, PagerDuty)
  • Communication and escalation protocols
  • Runbook execution

Passing Score: 80% or higher to proceed to shadowing phase

Training Tips

  • Complete Pre-Work: Review any assigned materials before each training day
  • Participate Actively: Ask questions and engage in discussions
  • Take Detailed Notes: Create a personal reference guide as you learn
  • Practice After Hours: Reinforce learning by exploring tools independently
  • Connect with Peers: Learn alongside other new hires when possible
  • Request Clarification: Don't move forward if you don't understand something
